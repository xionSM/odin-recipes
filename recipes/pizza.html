Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

Contadina(R) Quick Pizza Crust

  • 1/2(12 ounce) can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Fleischmann's Easy Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 2 (.25ounce) envelopes FLEISCHMANN'S Pizza Crust or RapidRise Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon supgar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 1/3 cups very warm water (120 Degrees F to 130 Degrees F
  • 1/3 cup oil

Toppings

  • 1 (6 ounce) package HORMEL(R) Pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. For sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavors to develop while making crust. Freeze remaining paste .

  2. Step 2

    3. For crusts: Combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 minute). Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed.

  3. Step 3

    4. Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed. (If using RapidRiseÂ® Yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)

  4. Step 4

    5. Divide dough in half. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.

  5. Step 5

    6. For pizzas: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Top crusts with sauce, pepperoni and cheese.

  6. Step 6

    7. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
